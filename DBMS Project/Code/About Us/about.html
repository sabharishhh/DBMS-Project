    • Med Care: Our Story

    Certain legacies transcend time, leaving an indelible mark on the hearts of those who experience them. Med Care Hospital is one such legacy—a beacon of medical excellence and compassionate care. Since its humble beginnings, Med Care has been a trusted name in healthcare, offering generations of patients the sacred gift of healing, infused with empathy and dedication. The story of Med Care is one of growth, service, and an unwavering commitment to the well-being of the community


    Founded in 1960, Med Care Hospital began as a small clinic with just 10 beds and one dedicated doctor. It was established with a vision by Dr. John Mathew, a pioneer in medical service, who aimed to create a healthcare institution that provided quality medical care with a human touch. What started as a modest venture has now blossomed into a world-class, multi-specialty hospital known for its excellence in patient care.


    Today, We Are:

    Today, Med Care stands tall as a comprehensive healthcare hub, providing advanced medical services under one roof. With a capacity of over 500 beds, the hospital houses 40+ specialized departments staffed by more than 150 expert doctors and supported by a dedicated team of over 1200 healthcare professionals. We pride ourselves on having the latest medical technologies, including state-of-the-art MRI and CT scanners, fully equipped operating theaters, intensive care units, and a comprehensive diagnostic center.


    Med Care Hospital's dedication to cutting-edge medical technology is reflected in its infrastructure, which includes advanced imaging facilities like the Philips 3T MRI Scanner, GE Revolution CT Scanner, Siemens Digital Cath Lab, and Philips 4D Ultrasound. In addition, our hospital offers 24/7 pharmacy, laboratory, radiology, and emergency services, ensuring that patients receive seamless and efficient care around the clock.


    At Med Care, we don't just treat diseases; we care for people. Our ethos blends clinical excellence with a deep sense of empathy, ensuring that every patient who walks through our doors feels supported, comforted, and valued. For over six decades, we have been a trusted partner in health, offering a blend of quality, compassion, and innovation that touches lives and fosters healing.

    Our Values

    · Compassion

    At Med Care, compassion is not just a value—it is the foundation of our care philosophy. We understand that healing is a holistic process that involves not only addressing the physical ailments of our patients but also nurturing their emotional and spiritual well-being. Our healthcare professionals are trained to listen, to empathize, and to offer a comforting presence at every stage of a patient's journey. Whether it's a reassuring smile, a kind word, or going the extra mile to ensure comfort, we treat each patient with the same level of care and dignity as we would our own family. By creating an environment of trust and respect, we ensure that patients feel safe, valued, and supported during their time of need.


    · Innovation

    Innovation is the lifeblood of Med Care. We are committed to staying at the forefront of medical advancements, continuously integrating the latest research, technology, and best practices into our healthcare services. Our hospital is equipped with cutting-edge medical technology, including advanced diagnostic imaging, minimally invasive surgical tools, and state-of-the-art treatment facilities. But our commitment to innovation goes beyond technology—it’s about constantly refining our processes to provide faster, more efficient care while maintaining the highest standards of quality. We embrace change as a way to improve outcomes, enhance patient experiences, and offer treatments that are safer, more precise, and less invasive. At Med Care, innovation ensures that our patients benefit from the most advanced care available.


    · Patient-Centered

    At the heart of everything we do is our dedication to being patient-centered. We recognize that every patient is unique, with individual needs, preferences, and concerns. That’s why we believe in personalized care that respects each patient’s voice and choices. From diagnosis to treatment, we involve patients in the decision-making process, providing clear, thorough explanations and ensuring they are comfortable with every step of their care plan. Our multidisciplinary teams collaborate to create tailored treatment plans that address the specific medical, emotional, and practical needs of each patient. At Med Care, the patient is not just another case—we see the whole person, and our goal is to ensure that every patient feels empowered, heard, and respected throughout their healthcare journey.
